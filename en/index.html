When to Shoot

Each season offers a Mt. Fuji
you cannot photograph any other time.

Fuefuki's location at the foot of Mt. Fuji means the landscape changes dramatically with the seasons. Choosing the right month is half the photo.

Spring · April
Peach Blossom × Mt. Fuji
Fuefuki is one of Japan's largest peach-producing regions. For 2 weeks in early-to-mid April, the entire valley turns pink with snow-capped Mt. Fuji rising behind.
Summer · June – August
Green Vineyards & Golden Hour
Lush green vineyards stretching to Mt. Fuji's silhouette. Long days mean flexible scheduling. Best for golden hour sessions and sunrise at Twin Terrace.
Autumn · September – November
Harvest & First Snow
Vineyard harvest, ripe peaches, and the first snow returning to Mt. Fuji's peak. The most colorful season — and the photographer's favorite.
Winter · December – February
Snow Peak & Onsen Mist
Pure white Mt. Fuji at its most iconic. Cold, clear mornings reveal the mountain in dramatic detail. Pairs beautifully with onsen-side portraits.
Peach blossom sessions (early-to-mid April): book 6+ weeks in advance.
Sunrise sessions at Twin Terrace: book 2+ weeks in advance.
Standard sessions: book 1+ week in advance.
