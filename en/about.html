Philosophy

My approach to photography

01

Design before shooting

Before pressing the shutter, I define the goal. Good photos start with understanding how the subject wants to be seen.

02

Comfort creates authenticity

I build a relaxed atmosphere so subjects forget they're being photographed — that's when real expressions emerge.

03

One photo, real impact

A single strong image can change how the world sees you. That's what I aim for in every session.
